Help

Get immediate help if you or someone you know is in a crisis.

National Suicide Prevention Hotline

Call 1-800-273-TALK (8255); En español 1-888-628-9454

This free confidental crisis hotline is a available for anyone 24 hours a day 7 days a week.

People who are deaf or have a loss of hearing can contact via TTY at 1-800-799-4889.

For the crisis text line, text “HELLO” to 741741 for 24 hour service.

Find a Healhcare Provider or Treatment

Treatment for mental health can be therapy, medication or a combination of both.

Try your primary care providor for screenings or referrals to specialists.

Federal resources can provide help find low-cost health services. Try SAMHSA, HRSA, CMS or NLM.

National, state and county agenices can be a great informational service for mental health.

If you're a student or live near a college, univeristies may offer treatment options.
