Help

Get immediate help if you or someone you know is in a crisis.

National Suicide Prevention Hotline

Call 1-800-273-TALK (8255); En español 1-888-628-9454

This free confidental crisis hotline is a available for anyone 24 hours a day 7 days a week.

People who are deaf or have a loss of hearing can contact via TTY at 1-800-799-4889.

For the crisis text line, text “HELLO” to 741741 for 24 hour service.
